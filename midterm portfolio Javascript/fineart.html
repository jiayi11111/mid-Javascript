Time's Hourglass


The work is based on printmaking and character sketches, which combine sketch self-portraits,
white latex, crayons, water-based paint, and newspapers.
I was sparked to create it when my mother was showing me pictures of different periods
in my life. I wanted to present all my life turning points in one artwork,
which allows me to feel my life in the painting.
