Frida Kahlo


The clay Sculpture "Frida kahlo" intended to pay tribute to Frida's spirit and her refusal to yield to fate. As a girl, I have the same desire to be an excellent female artist as Frida did, so I did not hesitate to choose her when making this work. I created a 3 D image through clay, which can be more stereoscopic and intuitive to people. Laying her favorite flower on the top of the clay is my way of showing her blooming life, presenting a symbol of vitality.

Art and Life


The work made with wool and pasted buttons, and the girl in the artwork is me. I hope to use more artistic and creative techniques to express my uniqueness. In Asian culture, wool means not only warmth but also present bonds and connections. I want to convey to my audience through the use of wool how my feelings toward art are irreplaceable.


Material:Woolen thread, Buttons
